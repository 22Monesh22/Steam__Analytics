Everything you need to know about Steam Analytics Platform

What is Steam Analytics Platform?

Steam Analytics Platform is an advanced business intelligence system that processes massive gaming data from the Steam platform. It analyzes:

  • 50,872+ games with complete metadata and performance metrics
  • 14+ million users and their gaming preferences, behaviors, and patterns
  • 41+ million reviews for sentiment analysis and user feedback

The platform provides actionable insights for game developers, publishers, and market analysts to make data-driven decisions.
